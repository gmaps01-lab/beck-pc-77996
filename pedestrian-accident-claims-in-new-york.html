How does the process work if I'm injured in a pedestrian accident in New York City?
If you’re injured as a pedestrian in NYC, Beck, P.C. will start by providing a free consultation to review your case. If we take your case, we’ll handle communication with the insurance companies, gather evidence like police reports and surveillance footage, and consult with medical experts if needed. We’ll work to establish fault and the full extent of your injuries, then negotiate for a fair settlement. If a settlement can’t be reached, we’re prepared to take your case to court. Throughout, we keep you informed and answer your questions promptly.
What fees do you charge for handling pedestrian accident claims?
At Beck, P.C., we work on a contingency fee basis for pedestrian accident cases in New York. This means you pay nothing upfront. Our fee is a percentage of the compensation we recover for you, which is standard in New York personal injury law. If we don’t win your case, you owe us nothing. We’ll explain our fee structure in detail during your initial consultation so there are no surprises.
How long does it usually take to resolve a pedestrian accident claim in NYC?
The timeline for resolving a pedestrian accident claim can vary. Beck, P.C. works to move your case forward as efficiently as possible, but we always prioritize getting the best possible result over a quick settlement.
